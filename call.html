Call for Papers

DialRes-LREC26 focuses on the challenges and opportunities in dialectal NLP from a resource perspective; it seeks to bring together researchers from linguistics, computational linguistics, digital humanities, and related areas to share work on the creation, documentation, and exploitation of dialectal resources.

Workshop Topics

  • Development and evaluation of dialectal oral and textual resources
  • Orthographic normalisation and homogenisation
  • Dialects vs. standard language varieties in annotation frameworks
  • Cross-lingual transfer and model adaptation
  • Resource scalability issues and techniques
  • LLMs in dialectal resource development
  • Resources for/and applications supporting dialect revitalisation and preservation
  • Dialectal studies and teaching from a resource perspective
  • Working on dialect resources: academic, financial, legal, societal issues
  • Enabling dialect communities to develop resources
