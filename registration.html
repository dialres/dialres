Registration

Participants of DialRes-LREC26 are kindly asked to complete their registration in due time.

📣 Early-bird registration deadline: March 31, 2026 (23:59 AoE)

From April 1, 2026, late and onsite registration fees will apply.

In order for an accepted paper to be included in the workshop schedule, at least one author must register.

Registration for a workshop only is possible upon payment of the corresponding workshop fee. However, registration for one specific workshop does not include access to other workshops or tutorials, nor does it cover registration for the main conference. These must be selected and paid for separately, as applicable.
